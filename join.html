Other ways to get involved

MACC is open to all anarchists and those interested in anarchists ideas such as direct action, mutual aid, direct democracy, anti-oppression, and horizontalism. To learn more about how MACC operates, check out our core documents and our wiki.

Attending a monthly General Assembly is a great way to get connected to MACC working groups and projects! Check our calendar for upcoming General Assemblies.

You can also follow us on Facebook or Twitter to get updates about other MACC projects and events.
